# Security Policy

We take the security of RECARN-UQAM projects seriously and appreciate responsible
disclosure of vulnerabilities.

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues.**

Instead, report them privately so the issue can be addressed before it is disclosed:

- Email **Francois Gonothi Toure** at
  [toure.francois_gonothi@courrier.uqam.ca](mailto:toure.francois_gonothi@courrier.uqam.ca),
  or
- Use GitHub's [private vulnerability reporting](https://docs.github.com/en/code-security/security-advisories/guidance-on-reporting-and-writing-information-about-vulnerabilities/privately-reporting-a-security-vulnerability)
  on the affected repository, where enabled.

When reporting, please include as much of the following as you can:

- A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.
- Steps to reproduce, or a proof of concept.
- The affected repository, version, or component.
- Any suggested mitigation, if known.

## What to expect

- We will acknowledge your report as soon as we reasonably can.
- We will investigate and keep you informed of our progress.
- We will let you know when the issue is resolved.

We ask that you give us a reasonable opportunity to address the issue before any
public disclosure, and that you avoid accessing or modifying data beyond what is
necessary to demonstrate the vulnerability.

Thank you for helping keep RECARN-UQAM and its community safe.
